How I work

My design process
in four steps.

01

Understand the real problem

I start with research — user interviews, surveys and desk research — before touching Figma.

02

Design with purpose

Every screen, every decision connects back to a real user need. No guessing.

03

Build and prototype

From rough sketches to high-fidelity screens, I iterate with intent — testing assumptions early, keeping feedback loops tight and honest.

04

Refine with feedback

I test with real users, note what breaks, and iterate until the experience feels right.
